Job Summary

  • Position: Machine Learning Intern
  • Experience Required: 0 - 1 years
  • Location: ICPowai Campus
  • Skills: Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Image Processing, Computer Vision
  • Qualification: BTech / MTech

Job Description

As a Machine Learning Intern, you will work alongside a team of data scientists and engineers to develop, implement, and optimize machine learning algorithms and models. This role offers the opportunity to build practical skills in data analysis, machine learning, and software engineering while contributing to impactful projects within the company.

Responsibilities

  • Develop and assist in the design, development, and testing of machine learning models.
  • Conduct data preprocessing, feature engineering, and data analysis for model preparation.
  • Collaborate with teams to understand business needs and apply ML techniques to solve problems.
  • Assist in model evaluation, performance tuning, and optimization.
  • Write and maintain clean, efficient, and well-documented code.
  • Contribute to research on emerging ML techniques and tools.
  • Prepare and present reports and visualizations for stakeholders.

Qualifications

  • Strong understanding of machine learning concepts and algorithms.
  • Proficiency in Python, R, or similar programming languages.
  • Experience with ML libraries/frameworks like TensorFlow, Keras, scikit-learn, PyTorch.
  • Solid grasp of data structures, algorithms, and problem-solving techniques.
  • Knowledge of data preprocessing, feature engineering, and model evaluation.
  • Strong analytical, problem-solving, and communication skills.
  • Ability to work independently and collaboratively in a fast-paced environment.

Preferred Skills (Optional)

  • Experience with deep learning techniques.
  • Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS, Google Cloud, Azure).
  • Knowledge of version control tools (Git).
  • Experience with SQL and working with databases.
