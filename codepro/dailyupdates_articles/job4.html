ELITE OFF CAMPUS 2024 HIRING is a fresher’s hiring initiative to attract the best engineering talent across the country. An enthusiastic engineering candidate should not miss this opportunity to be part of an exciting journey with Wipro!

  • 10th Standard: 60% or above
