Andhra Pradesh WhatsApp Services

Summary

The Andhra Pradesh government has launched a WhatsApp-based governance service called "Mana Mitra" to provide 161 citizen-centric services to the public. This initiative aims to reduce the need for physical visits to government offices and make services more accessible through the messaging app.

Details

Launched on January 30, 2025, citizens can now access various services, including bill payments, certificate issuance, and bookings, by sending a message to the dedicated WhatsApp number 9552300009.

Inauguration

The service was formally inaugurated by State IT Minister Nara Lokesh at his residence in Undavalli, Amaravati, and is expected to expand to include more services in the second phase.

Key Features

  • The WhatsApp number 9552300009 has been allocated for this purpose.
  • Users can access services by typing "Hi" and following the prompts for the desired department.
  • The initiative is a result of an agreement between the Andhra Pradesh government and Meta, signed on October 22, 2024.
  • The service includes offerings from various sectors, such as Endowments, Energy, Municipal Administration, APSRTC, and Anna Canteens.
  • Plans to add 360 more services in the second phase.

Security Measures

The government has emphasized the importance of cybersecurity and has taken measures to protect citizens' data from cyber threats, including the use of special QR codes on certificates to prevent counterfeiting.
